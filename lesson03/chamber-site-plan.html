Lucban Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Richard Obnamia

WDD 130-B7

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a digital hub for businesses and entrepreneurs in Lucban,Quezon,Philippines. This site will promote local businesses, events and economic opportunities within the area. It should also serves as an information central for local business regulations, economic trends and government initiatives even for SMEs or small and midsize business entrepreneurs. It will be a resource that should encourage locals to support products or business within the area.

Scenarios

  • When will the next food bazaar event?
  • Where can I find resources when starting SMEs?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
