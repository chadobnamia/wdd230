Population of The Philippines in 2024

The population of The Philippines stood at 118.2 million in January 2024.

Data shows that the Philippines’ population increased by 1.8 million (+1.5 percent) between early 2023 and the start of 2024.

49.2 percent of the Philippines’ population is female, while 50.8 percent of the population is male.
