Hick's Law

Amazon

amazon.com

The principle that Hick's law applied to this website is "reduce the number of stimuli and get a faster decision-making process" by making the navigation into categories they are applying Hick' Law

Rule of Thirds

Apple

apple.com

Rule of Thirds is applied to this website showing the balance in the element. The principle that it is applying is making the element reside within the third of the page.

Visual Hierarchy

Flux Academy

flux-academy.com

This website is applying the principle of Visual Hierarchy wherein it is showing the elements or information that are more important in a hierarchal order.
